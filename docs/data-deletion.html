SmackTrack Data Deletion

How to delete your data

Delete your data from within the app

1. Open SmackTrack and tap the Settings gear icon (top right).

2. If signed in, tap Delete Account at the bottom of the Cloud Sync section.

3. Confirm deletion. This will permanently delete:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account
  • All shot history stored in the cloud
  • All settings synced to the cloud
  • All achievement data stored in the cloud

Delete local data

To delete all data stored on your device:

  • Individual shots: Swipe or tap the delete icon on any shot in the History tab.
  • All local data: Uninstall the app from your device. This removes all locally stored shots, settings, and achievements.
  • App storage: Go to Android Settings → Apps → SmackTrack → Storage → Clear Data.

Request deletion via email

If you cannot access the app or need assistance, you can request data deletion by opening an issue on our GitHub repository with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and the email address associated with your Google Sign-in. We will delete your data within 30 days.

What data is deleted

  • Cloud data (Firestore): Shot history, settings, achievements — permanently deleted immediately.
  • Firebase Auth account: Permanently deleted immediately.
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymized and aggregated — cannot be tied back to you. Retained per Google's standard retention policy.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Anonymous crash reports — cannot be tied back to you.
